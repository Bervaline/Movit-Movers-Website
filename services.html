OUR SERVICES




Packing Services

At Mopack, we understand that proper packing is the cornerstone of a successful move, therefore, we offer customized packing solutions for unique moves, ensuring that our customers belongings are packed according to their preferences and the nature of the items. We use high-quality packing materials like sturdy boxes, bubble wrap, and foam peanuts to protect our customer's possessions during transit. Our systematic labeling system simplifies the unpacking process, ensuring no misplacement. We also take special care for fragile items, such as glassware, electronics, artwork, and antiques, using additional padding and custom crating to ensure their secure packing. Our goal is to deliver your belongings in the same condition as the old one.


Loading Services

Our skilled loading crew uses the newest tools to securely handle objects of all shapes and sizes. To ensure your things' safety throughout transit, we take precautions and offer specialized handling for those that need special handling instructions.

Transportation Services

Mopack has modern fleet, experienced drivers, and real-time tracking system that ensure smooth transportation. We offer flexible scheduling options and expertise for long-distance moves, ensuring safe arrivals in all road conditions.

Unpacking Services

Our team offers comprehensive unpacking services, including complete organization, debris removal, assembly services for furniture, organization assistance, and customized solutions. We also provide assembly services for furniture and other items post-move.
